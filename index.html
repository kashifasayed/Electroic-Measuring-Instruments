Electronic Measuring Instruments

The instruments used to measure any quantity are known as measuring instruments.
If the instrument can measure the basic electrical quantities, such as voltage and current
are known as basic measuring instruments.
Electrical Units of Measurement are used to express standard electrical units alnog
with their prefixes when the units are too small or too large to express as a base unit.
The standard units of electrical measurement used for the expression of Voltage, Current and Resistance
Volt[V], Ampere[A] and Ohm respectively.
These electrical units of measurement are based on the International (metric) System,
also known as the SI System with other commonly used electrical units being derived from SI based units.
