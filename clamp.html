Clamp Meter

  • A clamp meter is an electric test tool that combines a basic digital multimeter with a current sensor.

  • Clamps measure current. Probes measure voltage.

    • 1)Current-sensing jaw.
    2)Tactile barrier (to protect fingers from shock).
    3)Hold button: Freezes the display readings. Reading is released when button is pushed a second time.
    4)Dial:(aka rotary switch).
    5)Display.
    6)Backlight button.
    7)Min Max button:On first push, display shows maximum input.
    On subsequent pushes, minimum and average inputs are shown.
    Works in current, voltage and frequency modes.
